# Blinkit Customer Preference Analysis (Conjoint Analysis)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer preferences for an online grocery delivery platform similar to Blinkit using Conjoint Analysis. The objective is to understand which product or service attributes influence customer choice the most.

Conjoint Analysis helps businesses design better service offerings by identifying the relative importance of different attributes such as delivery time, price, and discounts.

---

## Business Problem

Blinkit wants to understand what factors influence customer decisions when choosing a grocery delivery service.

Key questions:

* Do customers care more about delivery speed or price?
* How important are discounts?
* Which combination of attributes creates the most attractive service offer?

This analysis helps businesses optimize their service design and marketing strategy.

---

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is a simulated customer survey dataset.

Attributes included in the analysis:

* Delivery Time
* Delivery Charges
* Discount Offers
* Product Availability

The dataset contains customer responses evaluating different combinations of these attributes.

---

## Methodology

Step 1: Define Attributes and Levels
Identify key service attributes and their possible levels.

Step 2: Generate Customer Profiles
Create different combinations of service offerings.

Step 3: Collect Customer Preference Data
Simulated customer ratings for each service combination.

Step 4: Data Preparation
Convert categorical attributes into dummy variables.

Step 5: Regression Analysis
Run regression to estimate the importance of each attribute.

Step 6: Conjoint Analysis
Calculate utility values and determine which attributes drive customer preference.

## Tools Used

---

## Key Insights

Example insights from the analysis:

* Faster delivery time significantly increases customer preference.
* Discounts have a strong positive influence on purchase decision.
* High delivery charges negatively impact customer choice.
* Customers prefer a balance between quick delivery and reasonable price.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

* Blinkit should prioritize faster delivery options.
* Offering periodic discounts can significantly improve customer attraction.
* Delivery charges should remain competitive to avoid losing customers.
